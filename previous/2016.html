The Avengineers

Engineering Revue 2016


When the forces of evil and tyranny threaten the livelihood of students in the Faculty of Engineering, a brave few will rise from the shadows to fight them. They're all great when they work on assignments solo, but will they be able to complete this group project, save the Engineering school and still graduate on time? Since it's a comedy, yeah, probably, but you'll have to come see to know for sure!
